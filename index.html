Varieca variegata

Varieca variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Lemur, is a small primate found in Madagascar, recognized for its distinct coloration and social behavior in forested habitats.

Varieca rubra

The Varieca rubra, or Red Lemur, is another species native to Madagascar, known for its vibrant red fur and strong family bonds, often seen in groups.

Eulemur fulvus

Eulemur fulvus, known as the Brown Lemur, is a social primate native to Madagascar, characterized by its adaptability and varied diet of fruits and leaves.

Indri indri

The Indri indri, or Indri, is the largest living lemur, famous for its loud calls and unique appearance, primarily inhabiting Madagascar's rainforests.
