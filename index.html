Eulemur fulvus

Eulemur fulvus, known as the Brown Lemur, is a social primate native to Madagascar, characterized by its adaptability and varied diet of fruits and leaves.

Indri indri

The Indri indri, or Indri, is the largest living lemur, famous for its loud calls and unique appearance, primarily inhabiting Madagascar's rainforests.
